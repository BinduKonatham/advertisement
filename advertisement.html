About Us

“Levi’s brand is iconic because it’s trusted by consumers — trusted for innovation, quality,
                performance,durability, style, fit and more.Levi’s listens to its shoppers and empowers them
                to help guide the company.Levi’s has been killing it for a number of years now, and its recent 
                IPO now allows it to get some public validation for what it’s been doing right — and, it’s been
                doing plenty right.Levi’s isn’t just killing it in the denim segment, it’s killing it as a 
                manufacturer and retailer. Every Levi’s channel is growing, including its robust brick-and-mortar
                channel, in every single one of its geographies.Levi’s is teaching other brands how to go to market.”
